Cook Tasty Pancakes At Home Easily In No Time

We will be teaching you how to make tasty greek yogurt pancakes in this article with few ingredients which you can make ready in 15 mins.

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces greek yogurt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
